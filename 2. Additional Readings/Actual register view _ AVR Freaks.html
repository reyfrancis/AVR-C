clawsons advice is good if You are running the AVRStudio simulator (simulating Your codes execution on the PC rather than running it for real in the actual AVR)..

If You want to see what is really happening "in the AVR chip" then You need some additional hardware, eg. a JTAG dongle, connected between Your target system and the PC running AVR Studio.
